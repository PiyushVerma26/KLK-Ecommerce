KLK Ventures Pvt Ltd - KL Khanna Group Of Company

KLK Ventures, formerly known as “KL Khanna Gear Industries Pvt. Ltd.,” is renowned for manufacturing heavy industrial gears. Upon joining the organization, I was instrumental in its transformation into KLK Ventures Pvt Ltd. and further diversified our portfolio to include heavy engineering and energy conservation under my leadership. We introduced a battery manufacturing plant and an in-house testing facility in 2018. Leveraging my strong entrepreneurial skills in the energy sector, import-export, accounting, trading, strategic planning, and business development, KLK has emerged as a preferred energy partner for energy storage, efficient lighting, and innovative solar solutions in various government, PSUs, and private organizations. I am actively involved in providing innovative energy solutions for rural development, reflecting our commitment to sustainable progress and community welfare.

Vision

Our vision is to be a global leader in sustainable energy solutions, revolutionizing the way the world harnesses solar power. We envision a future where solar energy is not just an alternative but a primary source of clean, abundant, and accessible power.
